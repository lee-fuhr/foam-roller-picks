A quality foam roller is one of the most affordable and effective tools for muscle recovery, improving flexibility, and easing post-workout soreness. Budget models often deliver the same core benefits as expensive ones, just without the frills. When choosing, focus on density (firm enough to release knots but not so hard that it hurts), surface texture (smooth for general use, knobby for trigger points), and length (full-length for back and thighs, shorter for targeting small areas and portability). Not every budget roller is built to last: some flatten over time or feel too squishy to do real deep-tissue work.
